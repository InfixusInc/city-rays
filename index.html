About Us

We are a floral company that is uniquely designed to provide a range of high-quality custom floral arrangements, and wonderful decorations and gifts, for events and functions and peripherals. City Rays Florals emphasize excellent products and services in a timely & efficient manner to ensure complete customer satisfaction whilst creating and maintaining successful relationships with customers.

Flowers for

Birthdays & Gifts

We assist you to choose the perfect bouquet of eye-catching flowers to commemorate birthdays, and to convey special messages so you can better express your feelings with sincerity whilst spreading happiness by brightening the recipient's day.

Flowers for the

Wedding day

We work to ensure a picture perfect wedding by setting the mood and tone of your special day with a gorgeous big bunch of flowers with a perfect blend of beauty, color, style and elegance. We provide a range of uniquely designed flowers that will be cherished and are guaranteed to delight!

Our Services

We provide custom floral designs for all occasions including:

Corporate Events
We specialise in fresh, innovative and eye-catching floral arrangements that helps to create a beautiful, vibrant and lasting impression that perfectly reflects your business. We advise you on the appropriate choice of colors, styles and flower for both office and events.
Flower Designs
We design and create beautiful, innovative and stylish floral designs ranging from freshly-cut hand-tied, artificial or dried flowers to suit each customer's occasion. We take great pride in the self-expression of personal style and bring your events floral dreams to life.
Holidays & Occasions
We provide flowers to make your events special & unforgettable by creating festive atmospheres for social gatherings. We also cater for special occasions such as weddings, funerals and milestones such as anniversaries.
